Getting it down shouldn’t feel this hard

Most people don’t struggle with what happened. They struggle with how to say it.

You might be replaying moments, trying to remember what came first. Or stopping and starting because it doesn’t sound right yet.

That’s normal.

H.A.V.E.N. helps you move through it one step at a time, so everything lands where it should.

How it works

You begin where you are
Start with what you remember. No formatting. No pressure.

We help you slow it down
You’re guided with simple follow-up questions based only on what you said.

Your statement takes shape
Your words are organized into a clear timeline that’s easy to follow.

You review everything
Change, remove, or adjust anything before it’s finalized.

You decide what happens next
Export it, save it, or come back later. Nothing moves without you.

What this feels like

This isn’t about writing something perfect.

It’s about getting to a point where you can read it back and think, “That’s what happened.”

What this is not

H.A.V.E.N. does not take over the process.

It is not legal advice.
It does not report to police or outside systems.
It does not replace professional guidance.
It does not add details you didn’t provide.

Privacy and control

You are not handing your story over.

Nothing is sent anywhere automatically. Nothing is shared without your choice.

You stay in control of your words the entire time.

When this helps most

When you need to document something clearly.
When you want to prepare before speaking to someone official.
When you are making sense of everything after the fact.
